The operator manages NatsCluster and NatsBox resources in any namespace.

To verify the operator is running:

  kubectl -n {{ include "nats-operator.namespace" . }} get deploy {{ include "nats-operator.fullname" . }}-controller-manager

To create your first NatsCluster:

  cat <<EOF | kubectl apply -f -
  apiVersion: nats.crewlet.cloud/v1alpha1
  kind: NatsCluster
  metadata:
    name: my-nats
  spec:
    replicas: 3
    image:
      repository: nats
      tag: 2.12.6-alpine
    config:
      jetstream:
        enabled: true
        fileStore:
          pvc:
            resources:
              requests:
                storage: 10Gi
  EOF

Then watch it come up:

  kubectl get natscluster my-nats -w

For more examples see: https://github.com/crewlet/nats-operator
